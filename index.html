Design Philosophy

Old interfaces remembered, not copied.

These apps borrow the clarity of tangible desktop metaphors: cabinets for documents, cards for people, shelves for films, tapes for voice notes, and calendars for planning. Every reference earns its place by making the product easier to scan, understand, and use.

The result is nostalgic without clutter: crisp typography, direct actions, accessible contrast, responsive layouts, and download links that stay easy to find.
